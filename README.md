# insight
# City Analysis
Bangalore leads with 1,019 restaurants — the largest and most competitive market
		Delhi has 727 restaurants — second largest but underrepresented vs population size
		Goa, Udaipur, Kochi have the fewest listings — emerging or niche markets
		Gurgaon is the most expensive city at ₹1,610 avg cost — premium dining hub
		Vadodara & Kochi are the most affordable at ₹612–₹650 avg cost
	Kochi ranks highest in customer satisfaction despite being affordable

# Rating Leaders
 Barbeque Nation — highest avg rating at 4.4083 (quality + scale)
	 Taco Bell — strong at 4.1636 despite being international
		Wow! Momo — most branches but lowest rating among top 5 at 3.69
		More branches ≠ better ratings

 # Pricing vs Rating Paradox
   Expensive cities like Gurgaon & Delhi do not have the best ratings
		Affordable cities like Kochi & Indore score higher in customer satisfaction
